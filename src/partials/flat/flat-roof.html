TPO

A roofing system composed of recycled bottles, tires and wax-based polymers. Life expectancy of a TPO roof is 10-12 years. EPA regulations will not allow for TPO roofing systems to be discarded into landfills, nor can they be recycled.

  • In efforts to find desirable balance between inflammability and UV stabilization, the ASTM has allowed changes to the formulation 6 times since 2003
  • Fire related damages are hidden from the public eye by pushing the claim onto HVAC contractors or source initiated contractors
  • The ASTM allows manufacturers to use any backer material without disclosing its compounds or origins, making recycling impossible
  • Warranties are worthless

Building Envelope

p>Prior to the new energy standards, a tar and gravel roof could hit 180 degrees, putting a massive heat load on the HVAC system. Now that maintaining temperature is easier with cool roofing systems, the HVAC systems no longer need to run continually to maintain a comfortable interior temperature, however, you may still be wasting energy and overloading the HVAC unless you address humidity, which should be maintained at the ASHRE standard of 40%-60% maximum humidity. The best balance is at 45-50%.
