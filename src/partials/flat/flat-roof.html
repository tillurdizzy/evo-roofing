EVO Flat Roof Systems

EVO-1HP

Average Life Span: 10 years to Life of Building

The last roof you will ever need! PVC roofing membranes made with DuPont™ Elvaloy® KEE are among the most durable, fastest to install, and easiest to maintain in the industry. Building owners who want their PVC roofing to last as long as possible, or who need extra resistance to weather and chemical attack, can expect decades of problem-free service.

  • At least 51% Elvaloy KEE. ASTM D6754-2 Standard.
  • Plasticizer retention ensures the membrane remains flexible and workable indefinitely, even in extreme environments
  • Environmentally-friendly, contains no volatile organic compounds (VOC’s)
  • Superior solar heat reflectance, significantly reducing air conditioning costs.

EVO-2SP roofing systems provide a clean, quick and economical installation with high resistance to fractures, weather elements, extreme temperature ranges and many contaminants.

  • Enhanced solar heat reflectance and chemical resistance

TPO

Average Life Span: 10-12 years

A roofing system composed of recycled bottles, tires and wax-based polymers. Life expectancy of a TPO roof is 10-12 years. EPA regulations will not allow for TPO roofing systems to be discarded into landfills, nor can they be recycled.

  • In efforts to find desirable balance between inflammability and UV stabilization, the ASTM has allowed changes to the formulation 6 times since 2003
  • Fire related damages are hidden from the public eye by pushing the claim onto HVAC contractors or source initiated contractors
  • The ASTM allows manufacturers to use any backer material without disclosing its compounds or origins, making recycling impossible
