Edge Details

EVO-E1

For use on wall caps and non-draining edge

EVO-E1 fascia provides your single-ply roof system with the maximum protection against wind up-lift damage to 215 mph, exceeding all municipal high-wind building codes. Tested per ANSI/SPRI/FM 4435/ES-1 Standard to a design pressure of 290 lbs./ft2 to comply with the International Building Code. Covers can be made from a variety of colors or anodized finish.
