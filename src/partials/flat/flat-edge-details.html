Edge Details

EVO-E1

For use on wall caps and non-draining edge

EVO-E1 fascia provides your single-ply roof system with the maximum protection against wind up-lift damage to 215 mph, exceeding all municipal high-wind building codes. Tested per ANSI/SPRI/FM 4435/ES-1 Standard to a design pressure of 290 lbs./ft2 to comply with the International Building Code. Covers can be made from a variety of colors or anodized finish.

EVO-E2

For use on non-draining edge conditions in high wind load

EVO-E2 fascia system meets most municipal building codes requiring 110 mph installation. Tested per ANSI/SPRI/FM/ES-1 Standard to a design pressure of 200 lbs./ft2. Complies with the International Building Code. Covers can be made from a variety of colors or anodized finish.

EVO-E3

For use on draining edge conditions in high wind load

EVO-E3 fascia system meets most municipal building codes requiring 72 mph installation. Tested per ANSI/SPRI/FM/ES-1 Standard to a design pressure of 210 lbs./ft2. Complies with the International Building Code. Covers can be made from a variety of colors or anodized finish from 3.75" to 8" in height.

EVO-E4

For use on draining edge conditions in standard wind load

EVO-E4 is specifically designed to simplify the installation process and save cost. Comprised of 22 gauge cleats and 24 gauge cover. Tested per ANSI/SPRI/FM/ES-1 Standard to comply with the International Building Code. Available in heights from 3.75" to 8". Limited colors in white, tan and grey.

NEVO-E5

For use on curbs and specific draining conditions

EVO-E5 fascia system meets most municipal building codes requiring 110 mph installation. Tested per ANSI/SPRI/FM/ES-1 Standard to a design pressure of 200 lbs./ft2. Complies with the International Building Code. Covers can be made from a variety of colors or anodized finish.

EVO-E6

For use on curbs and specific wall conditions

EVO-E6 high strength two piece counter flashings made from 24 gauge steel sheets. Available in a variety of configurations, colors and sizes.
