-- The Short Version --

The last roof you will ever need! PVC roofing membranes made with DuPont™ Elvaloy® KEE are among the most durable, fastest to install, and easiest to maintain in the industry. Building owners who want their PVC roofing to last as long as possible, or who need extra resistance to weather and chemical attack, can expect decades of problem-free service.


Not all Elvaloy is the same!

Evolution Roofing's EVO-1HP falls under the ASTM D6754-2 Standard, not the more common ASTM D4434. The D6754-2 is a stricker standard that specifies the exact formulation of ingredients, with Elvaloy® KEE being at least 51%.


-- The Long Version --

The EVO-1HP SinglePly Roof membrane with Elvaloy® KEE offers a level of endurance and performance that is well beyond other single ply roofing membranes. The initial installation cost is very competitive, and the proven long term durability provides long lasting value. Elvaloy enhances the performance and extends the useful life of polyvinyl chloride (PVC) products. Because of its high molecular weight, Elvaloy does not migrate out of the PVC membrane, improves low-temperature performance and helps to keep the membrane pliable and weldable.
